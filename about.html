About Us

The story behind Dur-e-Yateem and our passion for every child.

Our Mission & Story

Dur-e-Yateem was founded with a single, unwavering belief: every child deserves a chance. Located in the heart of Islamabad, Pakistan, our orphanage has become a safe haven for children who have lost their parents or been abandoned.

We go beyond providing basic necessities. Our dedicated team of caregivers, teachers, and volunteers work together to create a nurturing environment where children receive quality education, nutritious meals, medical care, and — most importantly — the emotional support they need to grow into confident, capable individuals.

From our humble beginnings in a small rented house, Dur-e-Yateem has grown into a thriving community. Today, we care for dozens of children, providing them with schooling, after-school tutoring, recreational activities, and life-skills training. Our vision is a world where no orphan is left behind.
